Updates all dimensions to match some proportions with the actual surface width and height. Ball is 1/12 of screen width wide and tall; Pad is 1/15 of screen width tall and 1/15 of width wide. Bricks have a proportion of 3:1, and are 1/7 of screen width wide. The ball is positioned at the center of the screen.
